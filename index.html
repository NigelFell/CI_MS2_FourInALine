How to Play

  1. Point the cursor at the top of the column you wish to drop your counter in.
  2. Left click to drop your counter.
  3. Your opponent then takes their turn.
  4. When four counters are connected vertically, horizontally or diagonally you win.
  5. Click "New Game" to play again, click again to change the starting colour.
